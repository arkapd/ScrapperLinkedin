### 🛑 Spam Blocklist

#### 1. Forbidden Keywords
- `registration fee`
- `pay to join`
- `work from home 50k` (unrealistic earnings)
- `guaranteed placement`
- `MLM`, `network marketing`
- `investment required`
- `form filling`, `ad posting`, `SMS sending`
- `urgent hiring for airport` (common scam pattern)

#### 2. Blocked Domains
- `gmail.com` (Main source of consultancy spam)
- `yahoo.com`
- `hotmail.com`
- `rediffmail.com`
- `outlook.com` (unless verified)

#### 3. Low-Quality Patterns
- **Excessive Phone Numbers**: More than 3 phone numbers in a single post.
- **Short Duration**: Posts with less than 50 characters of description.
- **Urgency**: Excessive use of `URGENT`, `IMMEDIATE`, `Hurry Up`.
