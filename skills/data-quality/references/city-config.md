### 📍 City Configuration & Quirks

#### Pune (Primary)
- **Status**: Home base.
- **Quirks**: High concentration of IT Parks (Hinjewadi, Magarpatta). 
- **Keywords**: `Hinjewadi`, `Magarpatta`, `Viman Nagar`, `Kharadi`.

#### Delhi / NCR
- **Status**: Massive market.
- **Scope**: Includes `Noida`, `Gurgaon`, `Faridabad`, `Ghaziabad`.
- **Quirks**: High concentration of operations and startup roles.

#### Bangalore
- **Status**: Tech Capital.
- **Quirks**: Prioritize `Developer` and `Data Science` roles.
- **Keywords**: `Whitefield`, `Electronic City`, `Koramanagala`, `HSR Layout`.

#### Hyderabad
- **Status**: Operations & Tech Hub.
- **Scope**: Includes `Secunderabad`, `Cyberabad`, `Hitech City`.
- **Quirks**: Strong mix of US-based healthcare and operations support.
